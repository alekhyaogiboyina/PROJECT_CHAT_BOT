BMI Calculator





Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure that helps assess whether a person has a healthy weight for their height. It is calculated by dividing a person's weight in kilograms by the square of their height in meters.
BMI is important because it provides a simple and quick way to identify if an individual is underweight, at a healthy weight, overweight, or obese. Knowing your BMI is crucial as it helps in identifying potential health risks related to weight. For instance, being overweight or obese increases the risk of developing conditions like heart disease, diabetes, and high blood pressure. On the other hand, being underweight can also lead to health issues like malnutrition and weakened immunity. Maintaining a healthy BMI can help reduce the risk of these health problems and promote overall well-being.
